# Loan Servicing & Delinquency Runbook (sample)

## Payment posting
- Payments post the same business day if received before 5:00 PM ET.
- A grace period of 15 days applies before a late fee is assessed.
- The late fee is 5% of the past-due payment amount or $25, whichever is less.

## Delinquency stages
- **30 days past due:** automated reminder calls and email begin.
- **60 days past due:** account is referred to the collections queue; a live agent makes contact.
- **90 days past due:** the loan is classified as seriously delinquent and reported to credit bureaus
  as such; loss-mitigation options (deferral, modification) are offered.
- **120 days past due:** the account is evaluated for charge-off.

## Hardship
- Borrowers may request a one-time payment deferral of up to two payments per 12-month period.
- Deferred payments are added to the end of the loan term; interest continues to accrue.
