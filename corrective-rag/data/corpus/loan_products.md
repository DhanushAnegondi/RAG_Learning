# Loan Products (sample)

## Personal loan
- Amounts from $2,000 to $50,000.
- Terms of 24, 36, 48, or 60 months.
- Fixed APR ranges from 8.99% to 23.99% based on credit tier.
- No prepayment penalty.

## Auto loan
- New and used vehicles up to 7 model years old.
- Maximum loan-to-value (LTV) is 125% including tax, title, and fees.
- Terms up to 72 months.

## Home equity line of credit (HELOC)
- Variable rate indexed to the prime rate plus a margin of 0.50% to 2.00%.
- Draw period of 10 years followed by a 20-year repayment period.
- Combined LTV may not exceed 85%.
